Amalfi Coast Package

3 Days / 2 Nights

Experience the picturesque views of the Amalfi coast, the historical Pompeii, Naples, and everything in between. This tour will depart from Rome at 7:30am and travel to Naples. You will stop to see the sights in Naples and then continue on to Pompeii. You will spend time seeing the sights of Pompeii and stop by a vendor market where you will see souvenirs made with a technique dating back to the Greek times that can be purchased and admired. After this you will continue on to the Amalfi Coast and check in to your hotel where you will have dinner. The second day will be spent in the coastal village of beautiful Amalfi. You have the option of taking a shuttle to a nearby private beach, or taking in the sights of the city. You may also arrange other excursions. You will have dinner and spend another night at the hotel. On the third day you will have breakfast and can have the morning to your leisure as the bus will depart Amalfi at 3:30pm. Traffic permitting you will be back in Rome at around 9:00pm.


Amalfi Coast Package Includes:
  • Accommodations in a 4 Star Hotel
  • Daily Buffet Breakfast
  • aily Buffet Breakfast
  • Two Dinners
  • An educational excursion in Pompeii
  • One Lunch in Pompeii
  • Transportation in an Air Conditioned Coach
  • Paid Entrance Fees to all historical sights
